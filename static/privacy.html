Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026 · Governed by the laws of Ontario and Canada

ParLevel is operated by shavi labs (“we”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the ParLevel inventory application (the “Service”). We comply with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Ontario privacy requirements.

1. Information we collect

ParLevel is designed for small businesses. We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service:

  • Account data: store name, store code, business type, currency preference, and a bcrypt-hashed PIN (we never store your PIN in plain text).
  • Inventory data: product names, categories, aisles, barcodes, quantities, par levels, and adjustment history.
  • Technical data: session tokens, optional “remember this device” tokens, IP address, browser user-agent, and login timestamps for security and audit purposes.
  • Consent record: timestamp when you accepted this policy during registration.

We do not collect customer personal information, payment card data, or employee HR records through ParLevel.

2. How we use your information

  • Authenticate you and isolate each store’s inventory (multi-tenant security).
  • Provide inventory tracking, low-stock alerts, and reorder features.
  • Protect against unauthorized access (rate limiting, audit logs, secure sessions).
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations.

3. Legal basis & consent

Under PIPEDA, we rely on your express consent at registration and on legitimate business purposes necessary to deliver the Service. You may withdraw consent by deleting your account, subject to legal retention requirements.

4. Security measures

  • Encryption in transit: all data is transmitted over TLS/HTTPS in production.
  • PIN protection: PINs are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12); we cannot recover your PIN.
  • Session security: HttpOnly, Secure (production), SameSite cookies; cryptographically random tokens.
  • Access control: each store’s data is isolated by business ID; no cross-store access.
  • Brute-force protection: login rate limiting after repeated failed attempts.

Note on “end-to-end encryption”: ParLevel encrypts data in transit and at rest on our servers. Because inventory is processed server-side for sync across devices, we do not claim client-only end-to-end encryption. Your PIN is never stored on your device.

5. Data retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Sessions expire after 30 days (or 90 days if “Keep me signed in” is enabled). Login attempt logs are retained for security monitoring. When you delete your account, all store and inventory data is permanently removed from our database.

6. Your rights (PIPEDA)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information — use “Download my data” in Account & privacy.
  • Correct inaccurate information — update items or store details in the app.
  • Withdraw consent and delete your account — available in Account & privacy with PIN confirmation.
  • Challenge compliance — contact us below; you may also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

7. Cross-border processing

If the Service is hosted outside Canada (e.g., cloud providers in the United States), your data may be processed in those jurisdictions. We require comparable protection and only use providers that maintain appropriate safeguards.

8. Cookies & local storage

  • Session cookie: required for authentication.
  • Remember-device cookie: optional, only set when you choose “Keep me signed in”.
  • Local storage (browser): optional store-code preference only; never your PIN.

9. Children

ParLevel is a business tool not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact
